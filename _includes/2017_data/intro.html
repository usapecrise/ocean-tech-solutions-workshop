About

The APEC Workshop: Launch for Ocean Tech Solutions will bring together government officials, industry leaders, researchers, and innovators to explore emerging technologies that address marine debris, strengthen maritime domain awareness, and support efforts to combat illegal, unreported, and unregulated (IUU) fishing. Through regional collaboration and knowledge sharing, participants will identify innovative solutions that contribute to a more sustainable, secure, and resilient blue economy across the Asia-Pacific.
