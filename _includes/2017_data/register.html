APEC Workshop: Launch for Ocean Tech Solutions

August 19, 2026 · Dalian, China

Join government officials, industry leaders, researchers, and innovators from across APEC economies to explore cutting-edge ocean technologies that address marine debris, strengthen maritime domain awareness, and support efforts to combat illegal, unreported, and unregulated (IUU) fishing.

Economies are strongly encouraged to consider participants with the following backgrounds to ensure that the intended capacity building objectives of the workshop are achieved.

  • Professional background: Participants may include policymakers and practitioners involved in ocean, fisheries, environmental, or maritime issues, including those responsible for implementing policies related to marine debris and waste management, fisheries enforcement, or maritime domain awareness.
  • Skills and sectoral expertise: Registrants are expected to have a working knowledge of ocean technologies, maritime systems, or related technical and policy areas, as well as an interest in the development and use of innovative technology solutions to address ocean challenges. Additional knowledge of their economies’ priorities related to marine sustainability, fisheries management, or blue economy development is also welcomed.
  • Language skills: Registrants should be proficient in English, as all discussions and workshop materials will be in English.
