We welcome your participation in the APEC Workshop: Launch for Ocean Tech Solutions, bringing together policymakers, private sector innovators, researchers, and technical experts to explore innovative technologies that address marine debris, strengthen maritime domain awareness, and combat illegal, unreported, and unregulated (IUU) fishing.

If you are interested in attending, please complete the registration form above. Economy participants should coordinate accreditation through their economy's Delegation Accreditation Officer (DAO). Participants who are not attending as part of an APEC economy delegation should contact the Project Overseers for accreditation assistance.
